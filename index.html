He-man

About He-man

He-man is a prince who can transform into strong superhero by the power of his sword.Together with his friends he is fighting against the evil Skeletor.

He-man and his friends

He-man's Characteristics

  1. He's fighting against evil
  2. He's very strong
  3. He has a pet cat which can transform into armored tiger
  4. He's using magic sword

He-mans friends

He has many friends who are helping him in his fight against evil. Some of them are: Man at arms, his mentor, Teela, a female warrior, Sorceress, the guardian of the Castle Grayskull, Orko, a funny character from another dimension.
